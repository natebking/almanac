## Before keys are released

- Confirm signed lease is saved in the property Drive folder.
- Confirm first payment and security deposit are documented.
- Confirm utility transfer instructions were sent.
- Confirm tenant has access codes and parking instructions.
- Confirm filter size and appliance notes are included in the welcome packet.

## Move-in day

- Walk through exterior doors, windows, locks, garage, gate, and mailbox.
- Confirm smoke detectors and carbon monoxide detectors are working.
- Record any tenant notes in Almanac.
- Save photos in the property Photos folder.
